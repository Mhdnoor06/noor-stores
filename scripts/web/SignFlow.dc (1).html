MASTER SERVICES AGREEMENT
Agreement No. {{ ecNum }}

1. Parties & Recitals

This Master Services Agreement (the "Agreement") is entered into as of the Effective Date by and between Northgate Legal Ltd., a company registered in England and Wales (the "Provider"), and Acme Corporation, a Delaware corporation (the "Client"), each a "Party" and together the "Parties".

WHEREAS the Provider is engaged in the business of providing professional advisory and managed services; and WHEREAS the Client wishes to engage the Provider on the terms set out herein.

2. Scope of Services

The Provider shall perform the services described in each Statement of Work executed by the Parties (the "Services"). Each Statement of Work shall be governed by and incorporated into this Agreement. The Provider shall use commercially reasonable efforts to meet the timelines set forth therein.

3. Fees & Payment

In consideration of the Services, the Client shall pay the Provider the fees set out in the applicable Statement of Work. The total contract value shall not exceed £120,000 in any twelve-month period. Invoices are payable within thirty (30) days of receipt.
